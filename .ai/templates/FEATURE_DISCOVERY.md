## Recommended interview order

1. Problem, motivation, and measurable outcome
2. Users, stakeholders, permissions, and affected parties
3. Primary workflows and failure/recovery scenarios
4. Scope, non-goals, and release boundary
5. Domain rules, data ownership, lifecycle, and retention
6. Interfaces, integrations, compatibility, and migration
7. Security, privacy, reliability, performance, and accessibility
8. Operations, observability, deployment, rollback, and support
9. Architecture and implementation choices that require product or owner input

Do not ask about branches that cannot materially affect scope, behavior, risk, architecture, or acceptance.
